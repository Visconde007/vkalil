Code Proactively for Equity


By being a coder, I am in a place of priviledge. I have the ability to make online systems, products, and processes. Coders are the architects of a virtual world and I believe coders need to be held responsible for it.

This is why I code and design not only for equality, but also for equity. I code to uplift those who have been marginalized in the real world and online. I believe all coders have a duty to use creations to empower their users.

I firmly believe programmers must code proactively to benefit all communities and fight against the tide of misinformation and more, instead of reacting when things get bad. Programs can have a much more multifaceted impact than what coders believe on their side of the screen.
